Our Vision

At Kala Sanskruti Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, we envision a world where art transcends boundaries, empowering individuals to express their unique creativity and emotions freely. Through our unwavering commitment, we aspire to nurture and inspire the artistic souls of every child, fostering a society that values and celebrates art in all its forms. We strive to create an inclusive and supportive environment, where aspiring artists are encouraged to explore their potential and pursue their passion fearlessly. Our vision is to cultivate a generation of artists who enrich the world with their imagination, innovation, and the profound beauty of their artistic expressions.

Vitthal S. Paithankar

Founder Kala Sanskruti Shikshan Prasarak Mandal and Co-Founder Rangresha School Of Arts & Design

Our Leaders

Vitthal S. Paithankar

Founder Kala Sanskruti Shikshan Prasarak Mandal and Co-Founder Rangresha School Of Arts & Design

Art Teacher Diploma (A.T.D.),
Bachelor of Fine Arts (B.F.A.) , Master of Fine Arts (M.F.A.)

"Art, a Birthright for All" - With this simple motto, my journey towards dedicating my life to art began. Having experienced the hardships of poverty during my early years, I realized that art provides a unique freedom of expression like no other. As I matured, I made it my mission to help children achieve their dreams through the power of art. In the year 2000, fueled by an innate enthusiasm and love for art, I established "Kala Sanskruti Shikshan Prasarak Mandal" in Aurangabad. It all started with providing home tuitions to just two students, and now we have nurtured many more budding artists.

Gokul N. Paithankar

Founder Rangresha School Of Arts & Design

Art Teacher Diploma (A.T.D.),
Bachelor of Fine Arts (B.F.A.) from Sir J. J. School of Art, Mumbai

My journey in the world of arts began with the motto "Every Child Is an Artist," aiming to inspire and unlock the creative potential within every child. I pursued my studies in ATD (Art Teacher's Diploma) and BFA (Bachelor of Fine Arts) at Sir J. J. School of Art, Mumbai. In 2016, upon returning to Aurangabad, I established the brand "Rangresha" under the umbrella of "Kala Sanskruti Shikshan Prasarak Mandal." Since then, our efforts have borne fruit, and we are proud to share that over 550+ students have successfully secured admissions in Fine Arts Entrance Exams and received National Scholarships. Our mission to nurture young talents and provide them with opportunities in the field of Fine Arts continues to flourish.
